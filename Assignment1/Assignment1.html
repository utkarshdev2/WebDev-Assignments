How to make Tomato pasta

Ingredients

Cherry tomatoes, Pasta of any kind, Pasta water, Chilli flakes, salt, pepper, garlic cloves, basil leaves, olive oil

Preparation

Cut tomatoes in half, mince garlic cloves, break off some basil leaves, boil water in a pot

Process

Salt boiling water generously;put pasta in water and cook till almost done; In a pan heat up olive oil; add garlic and chilli flakes and let them simmer until fragrant; Add tomatoes, toss around until they soften, then squish them. Add the basil leaves and mix the juices with the leaves; Add salt and pepper. Finish with pasta water to desired consistency. Add in the pasta and toss until mixed well.

Optional modifications

Italian seasoning may be used instead of basil for convenience; Top with your favourite cheese. Cream or milk may be added to the sauce for texture and taste. Pre-fry a sausage and add it to the sauce for protein
